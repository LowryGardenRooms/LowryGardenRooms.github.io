1.5m x 1.5m Garden Office Pod

Need a quiet space to work from home without sacrificing your garden? Our compact, budget- friendly Garden Office Pod is the ideal solution. Fully insulated with 75mm insulation in the floor, walls and roof, it’s designed for comfortable, year-round use — keeping you warm in winter and cool in summer.

Your Pod Comes Complete With:

  • Full insulation throughout.
  • Plasterboarded interior, ready for skimming.
  • First fix electrics ready for connection and second fix.
  • Modern external cladding.
  • Professionally installed ground screw foundations.
  • A fully glazed anthracite grey uPVC door for natural light and a contemporary finish.
