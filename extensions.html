Extensions

Timber Framed Extensions

A flexible, cost-effective build method suitable for a wide range of extensions. Timber frame construction allows for design freedom while remaining compliant with permitted development regulations.

SIP Panel Extensions

Looking for a warmer, more energy-efficient extension built in less time? SIP panels offer a modern, high-performance alternative to traditional timber construction.

With excellent insulation and outstanding thermal performance, SIP extensions keep the heat in and energy bills down. Because the panels are precision-made in a factory, installation is faster, meaning less time on site and less disruption to your home. The result is a stronger, more airtight structure that feels warmer in winter, cooler in summer, and comfortable all year round. Perfect for homeowners who want maximum energy efficiency and a quicker, smarter build.

Energy Efficiency

Our timber frame and SIP extensions are built to exceed building regulations, delivering excellent insulation and long-term energy savings.

Faster Build Times, Less Disruption

Our modern construction methods allow us to complete extensions significantly faster than traditional masonry builds.

  • Timber frame and SIP structures are erected quickly on site.
